PRS & EVH Guitars

About PRS Guitars

PRS (Paul Reed Smith) guitars are renowned for their craftsmanship, playability, and beautiful finishes. Used by many professional musicians, PRS guitars are known for their versatility and rich tone, making them a favorite in genres from rock to jazz.

EVH: The Legacy of Eddie Van Halen

EVH guitars, inspired by the legendary Eddie Van Halen, are designed for speed, comfort, and iconic sound. These guitars feature unique hardware and finishes, and are a staple for players seeking that classic rock edge.
